About

Hi, my name is Volodymyr.

From the early years I felt a desire for creation thus I started from soldering my first radio transmitter. Now I am a software developer. I live and work in Kyiv, Ukraine. Apart from that, I play bass guitar in a punk rock band called Time To Live and also regularly play basketball in an amateur team.
